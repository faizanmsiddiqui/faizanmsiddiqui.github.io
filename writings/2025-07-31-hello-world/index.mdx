# Hello, World!

:::info

This is my first article, and I am excited to share it with you. It serves as an
introduction to my writing journey and what you can expect in the future.

:::

![featured-image.png](./featured-image.png)

As is tradition in the world of computer programming, the first program we write
is often a simple
["Hello, World!"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%22Hello,_World!%22_program) to
start our journey. So, what better title to use than this one to begin my
writing journey?

{/* truncate */}

## Motivation

I have been thinking about starting to write for a long time, but I never got
around to it for one reason or another. So, let's not waste any more time and
get started, because the longer we wait, the harder it becomes. As the Chinese
proverb says, "The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best
time is now." So here I am, beginning my writing journey.

## Some Background

I plan to write about various topics related to
[Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Science,_technology,_engineering,_and_mathematics),
particularly around my background and experience in Software Engineering,
Networking and System Administration, Infrastructure, DevOps, Cloud Computing,
Information Security, and Project and Product Management, as well as my newfound
interests over time. These days, I am exploring Artificial Intelligence (AI),
Gamification, and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and how they can be
integrated with Information Security, Privacy, and Software Engineering in both
academic and applied ways.

## Writing Style

The topics will be a mix of technical and non-technical articles, blog-style
posts, how-to guides, personal interests and notes (which I believe can be
helpful for others as well), opinions, tips, tricks, and tutorials. I will try
to mention the expected audience for each article to clarify who might find it
most useful. Apart from that, I will keep the writing style simple and concise,
just as I prefer to read myself.

## Final Words

That's all for now. I hope you will find the content useful and informative. If
you have any feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out to me via
[Email](mailto:imfsiddiqui@yahoo.com) or
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/imfsiddiqui). I would love to hear from
you. Hope you enjoy the content, happy reading!
