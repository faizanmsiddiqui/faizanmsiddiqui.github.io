# Hello, World!

:::info

This is my first article, and I'm excited to share it with you. It's an
introduction to my writing journey and what you can expect moving forward.

:::

![Hello, World!](./featured-image.png)

As is tradition in the programming world, the first program we write is often a
simple **"Hello, World!"**<span className="hide-footnote-mark">[^hello]</span>
to kick things off. So what better title to use than this one to start my own
writing adventure?

{/* truncate */}

## Motivation

I've been thinking about writing for a long time, but I never got around to it
for one reason or another. So let's not waste any more time because the longer
we wait, the harder it gets. As the Chinese proverb says:

> _"The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is
> now."_

So here I am, finally beginning my writing journey.

## Some Background

I'm planning to write about all sorts of topics related to **Science,
Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)**[^stem], especially drawing
from my background in Software Engineering, Networking and System
Administration, Infrastructure, DevOps, Cloud Computing, Information Security,
and Project and Product Management.

Lately, I've been exploring Artificial Intelligence (AI), Gamification, and
Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and I'm curious about how they can blend with
Information Security, Privacy, and Software Engineering in both academic and
practical ways.

## Writing Style

You can expect a mix of technical and non-technical articles, blog-style posts,
how-to guides, personal notes (which I think might help others too), opinions,
tips, tricks, and tutorials.

Check out [**Writings**](/writings) for more details. I'll try to mention the
intended audience for each piece so it's clear who might find it useful. Other
than that, I'll keep my style simple and to the point, just the way I like to
read myself.

## Final Words

That's all for now. I hope you find the content useful and interesting. If
you've got feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out to me via
[**Email**](mailto:imfsiddiqui@yahoo.com) or
[**LinkedIn**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/imfsiddiqui). I'd love to hear from
you.

Enjoy the content, and happy reading!
