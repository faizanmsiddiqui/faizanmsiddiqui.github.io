As is tradition in the programming world, the first program we write is often a
simple **"Hello, World!"**[^hello] to kick things off. So what better title to
use than this one to start my own writing adventure?

## Motivation

I've been thinking about writing for a long time, but I never got around to it
for one reason or another. So let's not waste any more time because the longer
we wait, the harder it gets. As the Chinese proverb says:

> _"The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is
> now."_

So here I am, finally beginning my writing journey.

## Some Background

I'm planning to write about all sorts of topics related to **Science,
Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)**[^stem], especially drawing
from my background in Software and System Engineering, DevOps and
Infrastructure, Full-Stack Web Development, Information Security, Embedded
Systems, Robotics, Multimedia, and Project and Product Management.

## Writing Style

You can expect a mix of technical and non-technical articles, blog-style posts,
how-to guides, personal notes (which I think might help others too), opinions,
tips, tricks, and tutorials.

I'll try to mention the intended audience for each piece so it's clear who might
find it useful. Other than that, I'll keep my style simple and to the point,
just the way I like to read myself.

## Final Words

That's all for now. I hope you find the content useful and interesting. If
you've got feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out to me via email at
[**faizanmsiddiqui@yahoo.com**](mailto:faizanmsiddiqui@yahoo.com) or on
[**LinkedIn**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/faizanmsiddiqui). I'd love to hear
from you.

Enjoy the content, and happy reading!
